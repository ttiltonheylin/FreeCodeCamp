about

A copper cloth is firm enough to scrub cast iron while being gentle enough to polish crystal. This type of versatilty is the foundation of our hospitality philosophy. We at copper cloth believe without versatilty and shared experience one cannot be truly hospitable. We empasize many virtures throughout our practices to ensure a seamless and understandable approach to our ideas. By infusing all our actions with versatility, organization, and energy we create, repeatable and conistent, actionable steps to grow into the future.
